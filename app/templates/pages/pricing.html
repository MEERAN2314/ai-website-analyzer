Frequently Asked Questions

How does the 30-day free trial work?

All plans come with a 30-day free trial. No credit card required. You get full access to all features of your chosen plan for 30 days absolutely free.

What happens after the trial ends?

After your 30-day trial, you'll have a 7-day grace period to add payment information. If you don't upgrade, you'll be automatically moved to the Free plan.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When you upgrade, you'll get another 30-day free trial of the new plan.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans. Payment processing will be available after your trial period.
